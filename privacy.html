Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Vocabry ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

Important: This app is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Data Type Purpose
Email address Account authentication via Google Sign-In
Display name Personalization of the app experience
Learning progress Syncing study data across your devices

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Data Type Purpose
Device information App optimization and troubleshooting
Usage statistics Improving app features and performance
Crash reports Identifying and fixing bugs

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: Enable vocabulary learning features, track your progress, and sync data across devices
  • Process Payments: Handle subscription purchases through Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix issues
  • Communicate: Send important service-related notifications

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and cloud services
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: For payment processing
  • Cloudflare: For content delivery (audio files)

These services process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored securely on Google Cloud Platform servers
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only

5. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Learning progress is retained for sync functionality
  • You can request deletion of all your data at any time
  • Upon account deletion, data is removed within 30 days

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your learning data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable optional data collection features

7. Children's Privacy

Vocabry is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect, use, or share personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. If you believe we have information from a child under 13, please contact us.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt-out of data sales. We do not sell personal information.

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EU residents have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. Our legal basis for processing is consent and legitimate interests.
