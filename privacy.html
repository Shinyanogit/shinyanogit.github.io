Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Vocabry ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and display name.
  • Learning Data: Your vocabulary learning progress, review history, and study statistics.
  • Purchase Information: If you subscribe to premium features, we process payment through Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the service
  • To sync your learning progress across devices
  • To process your subscription purchases
  • To improve and optimize the app experience

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Cloud Platform servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and analytics
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: For payment processing
  • Cloudflare: For content delivery

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your learning data

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us
